About

Born and raised in Toronto, Canada, I'm a freelance web developer and Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo. Design and technology are two passions of mine, and I channel my enthusiasm for both through my work.

I'm proficient with HTML, CSS, Sass/SCSS, and JS/JQuery; experienced with Bootstrap, Racket/Scheme, and Photoshop; and familiar with C, SQL, PHP, Python, Swift, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and InDesign. That said, I'm always eager to throw myself at new challenges, and dedicated to improving my skillset.

Aside from programming, music is a large part of my life, and I sing and play piano on top of listening to whatever songs capture my interest these days. I also write stories, read sci-fi novels, typeset notes in LaTeX, and play video games.
